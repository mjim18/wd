The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

Action-adventure game developed and published by Nintendo


The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild is a 2017 action-adventure game developed and published by Nintendo for the Nintendo Switch and Wii U consoles. The game is an instalment of The Legend of Zelda series..


Mode: Single-player video game

Designer: Eiji Aonuma

Awards: BAFTA Games Award for Innovation

Release date: 3 March 2017
